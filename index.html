Galia's Gambling Club

A Bondage Club addon that adds canvas games, dice and coin animations, trades, playful text effects, and a radial menu built for chat-room interactions.

What It Adds

Games In Chat

Play Snake, Slots, Tic-Tac-Toe, Battleship, Connect 4, and Blackjack from an in-canvas interface.

Radial Menu

Open GGC from the character dialog and choose games, dice, coins, ghosting, and trades with quick hover feedback.

Room Effects

Dice, coin tosses, item text, hearts, shocks, slaps, and other animations appear over the relevant character.

Install

Use the stable or beta script URL in your preferred Bondage Club addon loader or userscript manager.

  1. Copy the stable or beta script URL shown above.
  2. Add it to your Bondage Club addon loader, or create a userscript that requires it.
  3. Reload Bondage Club and enter a chat room.
  4. Open a character dialog and use the floating GGC radial menu.
