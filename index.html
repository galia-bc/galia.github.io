GGC Bank

The bank is a mobile-style interface for balances, money transfers, recent transactions, account permissions, auto-accept rules, and remote account control when another player has granted access.

Send money to compatible players.
Review recent saved transfers.
Configure bank and Findom permissions.
Use hidden GGC messages for remote account checks.

Radial Tools

Open GGC from a character dialog and use the radial menu for games, dice, coins, trades, bank access, and utility actions. It detects who has the addon and disables controls with useful hover feedback when a feature is unavailable.

One-die and two-dice rolls.
Coin tosses with room-visible motion.
Trade and bet flows with stake checks.
Room effects for playful actions.

Install

  1. Copy one of the script URLs.
  2. Add it to your Bondage Club addon loader or userscript manager.
  3. Reload Bondage Club and join a room.
  4. Open a character dialog and look for the GGC radial menu.
