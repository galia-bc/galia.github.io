Detailed guide

Install, play, trade, and manage GGC safely.

This guide explains the visible parts of Galia's Gambling Club: where to install it, how to open the radial menu, how games and trades behave, and what bank permissions mean before you allow another player to control anything.

Installation

Stable is recommended for everyday play. Beta receives new features first. You can use either URL in a Bondage Club addon loader, or choose one of the simpler methods below.

Stable: https://galia-bc.github.io/galia.github.io/club-gaming/stable/club-gaming.js
Beta: https://galia-bc.github.io/galia.github.io/club-gaming/beta/club-gaming.js

Tampermonkey or Violentmonkey (recommended)

Install Tampermonkey or Violentmonkey, create a new userscript, and paste the Stable template from the GitHub installation guide. Save it and GGC will start automatically whenever Bondage Club opens. A Beta template is available there too; only enable one GGC userscript at a time.

Bookmarklet (temporary)

Create a browser bookmark, paste this as its URL, then open Bondage Club and select the bookmark. It must be run again after every page reload.

Stable bookmarklet: javascript:(()=>{document.head.appendChild(Object.assign(document.createElement('script'),{crossOrigin:'anonymous',src:'https://galia-bc.github.io/galia.github.io/club-gaming/stable/club-gaming.js?v='+Date.now()}));})();
Beta bookmarklet: javascript:(()=>{document.head.appendChild(Object.assign(document.createElement('script'),{crossOrigin:'anonymous',src:'https://galia-bc.github.io/galia.github.io/club-gaming/beta/club-gaming.js?v='+Date.now()}));})();
  1. Add the script URL to your addon loader.
  2. Save the loader settings.
  3. Reload Bondage Club.
  4. Join a chat room.
  5. Open a character dialog and look for the GGC radial menu.

For multiplayer features, both players need a compatible GGC version. If a button is disabled, hover it to see why.

Radial Menu

The radial menu appears from a character dialog. It groups the addon features by context, so some options only appear on yourself, some on other players, and some only when the target has GGC loaded.

Games

Start single-player games on yourself or invite compatible players to multiplayer games.

Tools

Roll dice, flip coins, open trades, and use room-visible utility actions.

Bank

Open your bank account, or an accessible account when permission has been granted.

Hover feedback

Disabled buttons explain whether a feature is unavailable, missing GGC, or blocked by permissions.

Games

GGC includes several room-friendly games and single-player activities. Multiplayer games use invitations, and the target player must have the addon active.

  • Minesweeper: choose Small, Medium, Big, or Custom before playing. Left click opens a tile, right click marks a mine.
  • Snake: single-player score chasing with saved local scores.
  • Blackjack and Liar's Dice: multiplayer tables with invite and lobby flows.
  • Tic Tac Toe and Connect Four: quick two-player games from the radial menu.

Single-player games do not show turn prompts meant for multiplayer games.

Trades and Bets

Trades and bets both use contract-style screens, but they mean different things.

Trade

Both sides agree to terms that are applied when the trade is confirmed. A trade is not a wager.

Bet

Terms can be attached to a game. They apply according to the result of the bet.

Money, clothing, restraint, and ownership terms should be reviewed before accepting. If anything looks wrong, cancel before confirming.

Bank App

The bank is a phone-style interface for your GGC money, recent transfers, and account controls.

  • Send money: choose a player, enter an amount, and confirm the transfer.
  • Latest transfers: shows recent money movements saved by the addon.
  • Active account: lets you switch between your own account and accounts you are allowed to access.
  • Settings: controls auto-accept, Findom permissions, controller access, and account safety flags.

When auto-accept money is off, incoming money must be accepted or declined. If a transfer cannot be confirmed, the sender is notified and the money is returned.

Bank Permissions

Bank access is permission-based. Grant access only to players you trust, and review what each permission allows before enabling it.

Self Access

Controls whether the account owner can use their own bank controls. If disabled, the owner can open the bank but cannot operate it.

Auto Accept Money

Automatically accepts incoming transfers. If disabled, incoming money asks for accept or decline.

Enable Findom

Unlocks advanced account-control permissions for selected controllers.

Allow Settings Change

Allows approved controllers to change bank settings for the account.

View Bank Info

Lets a controller view total money and recent transfers for the account.

Send Money To Self

Lets a controller send money from the account to themselves.

Send Money To Others

Lets a controller send money from the account to other compatible players.

Revoke Access

Lets a controller remove access entries when allowed by the account settings.

Privacy and Safety

GGC uses local chat notices for important bank events, permission failures, completed transfers, and blocked actions. Remote account access is only shown when the account can be contacted and the required permission exists.

Ghosted players are treated as blocked by the addon: they should not receive GGC invites, bank notifications, transfer requests, money, or room actions from you.

Recent bank transfers are kept lightweight and limited so the addon can show useful history without storing unnecessary data.

If you do not understand a permission, leave it disabled until you do. Bank controls are meant to be explicit, visible, and reversible.

Troubleshooting

  • If the radial menu is missing, reload Bondage Club and join a room before opening a character dialog.
  • If multiplayer buttons are disabled, the target may not have GGC loaded or may be unavailable.
  • If remote bank access fails, the account owner may be offline, unreachable, missing GGC, or not granting access.
  • If bank buttons are disabled, check Self Access, Findom, and controller permissions.
  • If a transfer fails, the receiver may have declined it or the confirmation may not have arrived.
  • If the UI feels misplaced, try the reset option in GGC settings and reload the room.
